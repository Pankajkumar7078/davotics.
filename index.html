WorkShop

A workshop is a structured and interactive session designed to create an environment for meaningful work and to guide a group through a process that will lead to great outcomes. Workshops are designed to engage participants and foster their active involvement in the process. They are not training sessions where one person teaches everyone else in the room. Nor are they meetings where people give one another updates but rarely collaborate on a task with a structured approach.

Project Assistence

Project assistants are the glue that holds together many projects in virtually every industry. They commonly work under the supervision of project managers, but they may also take on some responsibilities independently. Project assistants help ensure that projects run smoothly by performing a variety of tasks. These might include scheduling meetings, making travel arrangements, ordering supplies, and handling other administrative duties. In addition to supporting their supervisors directly, they often act as liaisons between different departments or teams involved in the project.

Lab Setup

Setting a new lab is a challenging task. To make the process smooth, the first step of the lab setup is to define the purpose and function of the proposed laboratory. Whether it is an educational lab, research lab or analytical lab, every lab demands its own design and set of requirements. The research lab requires autoclaves, biosafety cabinets, freezers, incubators, and sanitized areas while the analytical lab needs air conditioning and controllable humidity.

Web Development

Web development refers to the creating, building, and maintaining of websites. It includes aspects such as web design, web publishing, web programming, and database management. It is the creation of an application that works over the internet i.e. websites.

Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is a form of marketing that leverages the internet and digital technologies, such as computers and smartphones, to connect with customers. More than running a sponsored Instagram ad to drive sales, it's a set of practices that interacts with customers at every stage of the buying journey.
