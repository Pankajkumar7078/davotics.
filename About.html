About Us

Vision & Mission
1- To provide quality services that exceeds the expectations of our esteemed customers.
2- To build long term relationships with our customers and clients and provide exceptional customer
services by pursuing business through innovation and advanced technology.

Goals
1- Increase the assets and investments of the company to support the development of services.
2- To build good reputation in the field of Training & Automation and become a key player in the
industry.

Core Values
1- We believe in treating our customers with respect and faith.
2- We grow through creativity, invention and innovation.
3- We integrate honesty, integrity and business ethics into all aspects of our business functioning.

With a zest of creating potential 21st-century Entrepreneurs, D@VOTICS intends to promote STEM
Education for K – 12 student and Engineering Students. STEM Education is a curriculum to educate
students in four domains of study – science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Since this
form of education fosters critical & innovative thinking, scientific, and analytical literacy, we
understand STEM education’s importance and provide students with exclusive STEM LABS, where they can
be trained in technical education and can upgrade their skills in different upgrowing fields like
Robotics, Engineering, Computer Science, AI etc with practical experience and in-hand activities and
exposure. D@VOTICS was founded in NOIDA in the year 2019 which provides Innovation labs and
workshops for schools and colleges across India. D@VOTICS has conducted workshops for 100k+ students
in India. In 2019, the first STEM & ROBOTICS lab of NORTH INDIA was inaugurated at Noida. D@VOTICS
in partnership with RAJSHREEE GROUP conducted a STEM workshop for 100+ schools across 24 Districts in UTTAR PRADESH.
